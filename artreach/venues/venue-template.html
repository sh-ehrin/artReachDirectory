Museum
The Metropolitan
Museum of Art

The Metropolitan Museum of Art is one of the world's greatest museums, with a permanent collection of more than two million works spanning five thousand years of world culture. From ancient Egyptian artifacts to modern masterpieces, the Met offers an unparalleled breadth of human creativity under one roof.

The Met's education department provides extensive resources for school groups, including guided tours aligned with New York State curriculum standards, hands-on workshops, and free educator materials. Group admission rates are available for public school students.

Student Programs

The Met offers free school group visits for NYC public school students, including self-guided and educator-led tours. Their "Met for Schools" program provides curriculum-connected gallery experiences for students at all grade levels. Pre-visit materials are available on their website.
